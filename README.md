# Yoberry 🍧

Welcome to **Yoberry** — your neighborhood frozen yogurt shop!

## About

Yoberry is a self-serve frozen yogurt experience with a wide variety of flavors and toppings. Mix and match to create your perfect cup!

## Flavors

- Original Tart
- Strawberry
- Mango
- Chocolate
- Vanilla Bean
- Coconut
- Matcha Green Tea

## Toppings

- Fresh fruit (strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, mango)
- Granola & muesli
- Mochi bites
- Gummy bears
- Sprinkles
- Brownie bites
- Honey drizzle
- Nutella swirl

## How It Works

1. Grab a cup
2. Swirl your favorite frozen yogurt flavor(s)
3. Top it off with your choice of toppings
4. Weigh your cup at the register — you only pay for what you take!
